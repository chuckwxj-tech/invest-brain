## 原文

The AI bottleneck keeps moving down the stack.

First it was chips. Then memory — CXL controller programs scrapped, KV cache spilling onto dedicated SSD shelves, contract prices up ~25% in a quarter.

Now it's power and permitting. A pipeline rejected twice in New Mexico. Australia moving to require data centers put back as much electricity as they draw.

And increasingly it's financing, with BlackRock owning 80% of a campus Meta will fill.

Each time one constraint clears, the next one sits further from anything a model can optimize its way out of.
