⭐ What to take into the field

Do not reach for CXL first. Prefix caching and paged attention give you more per dollar with zero new hardware. Look at pooling when your bottleneck is cache capacity, not compute, which is common on high-concurrency long-context serving. Measure TTFT and P99 before and after, since tail latency is where the tiering either pays off or quietly does not. And confirm your inference engine actually places KV on a NUMA or CXL node instead of pinning everything to HBM.
