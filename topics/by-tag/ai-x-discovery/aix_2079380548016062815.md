OSAT: CoWoS-like capacity to 15k / 50k / 85k wfpm by end-2026/27/28 (mainly for server CPU packaging).

CoPoS: Engineering stage; risk production early 2028, mass production 2029.

Demand:
NVIDIA: Rising allocations for Vera CPU (2027) and Rosa (2028).
AMD: Significant upside in 2027–2028 from Venice CPU (CoWoS-L-like at ASE).
ASICs: Google TPU, AWS Trainium ramping; Microsoft/Meta smaller scale. 2027 key ramp year for MTIA/Maia.

Intel EMIB-T: Incremental demand (e.g., MediaTek TPUv9); cheaper than CoWoS-L (~50% cost) but substrate capacity a limit.

Strong CoWoS ramp with customer demand pulling forward.
