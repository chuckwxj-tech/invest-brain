## 原文

New day, new $SHMD dilution SEC files. But let's check the details:
SHMD has 65,675,726 shares outstanding. 
Under the full dilution scenario the share count jumps to 130,090,622 shares. 
This represents a share count expansion of 98%; total float practically doubles. 
FY26 rev guidance >100M€: with the current share count each share represents 1.52€ in rev. 
If all options warrants and notes convert that number drops to 0.77€ in rev per share. 
If you use the upper end of the new order intake guidance of 150M€ as a future rev proxy the max diluted rev per share is 1.15€.  
For equity holders their slice of the business shrinks by half. 
For existing customers this corp process creates clear value creation.
Swapping millions in liabilities for equity and securing new convertible notes clears financials that delayed the last audits. 
This funding is, from a financial perspective, necessary to build the new fab in China to double production capa for AI server boards.
It also funds the engineering teams scaling the new ET process and glass substrate systems.
Future will tell if this China for China model really works. 
It's crazy dilutive for the stock but for the tech buyers it guarantees supply chain stability and provides next gen semi machinery that cuts factory energy use by 30%.
