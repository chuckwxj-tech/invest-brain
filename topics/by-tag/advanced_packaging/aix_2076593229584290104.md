## 原文

JNTC has signed a commercialization agreement with Japan’s Toppan for TGV glass substrates.

The two companies will collaborate on technology verification and mass production application for next-gen semiconductor packaging. 

Mass production target is 2027. JNTC aims to expand global supply chains through this partnership.

It has secured the industry’s first TGV glass substrate product lineup from 0.3 to 2.0mm thickness. The 2.0mm version uses a single sheet, achieving ~94 percent yield with solutions for micro-cracks, voids, and warpage.

The company is also planning domestic production line expansion to meet customer demand.

A positive step for Korean players in the growing glass substrate market for advanced packaging.
