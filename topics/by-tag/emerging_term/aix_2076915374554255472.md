THE HBM BONDING LEADER: HANMI SEMICONDUCTOR DELIVERS RECORD REVENUE AND 51.9% MARGINS

TLDR: Hanmi Semiconductor posted record quarterly revenue as demand surged for HBM TC bonders and MSVP equipment. The next growth leg is already being prepared: hybrid bonding, wider TC bonders and 2.5D AI packaging tools.

Q2 revenue rose 39.5% YoY to a record ₩251.2 billion ($168 million), while operating profit climbed 51% to ₩130.3 billion ($87 million).

Operating margin reached 51.9%. Compared with the previous quarter, revenue jumped 393.4% and operating profit surged 1,441.4%.

Hanmi attributed the improvement to rising investment by global chipmakers and stronger demand for HBM TC bonders and MSVP systems used in cutting, cleaning, inspection and placement.

The roadmap now moves beyond today’s HBM cycle. Hanmi plans to unveil a second-generation hybrid bonder prototype by year-end, launch a wide TC bonder in 1H27 and accelerate equipment supply for 2.5D AI semiconductor packaging.
