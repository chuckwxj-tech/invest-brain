## 原文

$ALAB

BOUNCE TIME SOON?

$ALAB is now down roughly 38% from its highs as the market continues rotating out of high-beta AI and semiconductor names.

The question:

Is the AI infrastructure trade breaking…

or is this simply another valuation reset on a company sitting at the center of a massive secular trend?

The current chart is starting to look interesting.

Price has pulled back into the $300 support zone, near previous breakout levels, while momentum indicators are becoming heavily washed out.

• RSI has fallen toward oversold levels
• MACD is showing one of its most stretched bearish readings since the IPO
• Bollinger %B suggests downside momentum may be getting exhausted

If ALAB can hold this level and reclaim key moving averages, this could become an accumulation phase rather than the beginning of a prolonged downtrend.

The fundamentals are why investors paid a premium in the first place.

Astera Labs sits directly in one of the biggest AI infrastructure bottlenecks:

CONNECTIVITY.

As AI clusters scale from hundreds of GPUs to thousands, moving data between chips becomes just as important as compute itself.

$NVDA builds the engines.

$ALAB builds the highways that allow those engines to communicate.

Their technology stack includes:

• PCIe retimers
• CXL connectivity solutions
• AI rack-scale connectivity
• Fabric switching technology

And the growth numbers show why the market became excited:

• Revenue growth has remained in the triple-digit YoY range as AI infrastructure demand accelerates
• Gross margins remain extremely strong at roughly 70%+, highlighting the high-value nature of their connectivity products
• Free cash flow has expanded rapidly as operating leverage improves, moving from early-stage investments into meaningful cash generation
• Net income growth is among the fastest in the semiconductor space, with expectations placing ALAB alongside names like $MU and $CRDO in earnings growth potential

The bigger AI infrastructure picture remains massive.

Data center demand is still accelerating:

• Global data center power demand is projected to rise roughly 50% by 2027 and potentially 165% by 2030 versus 2023 levels
• AI-ready data center capacity is expected to grow around 33% annually through 2030 in many forecasts
• AI workloads could represent roughly 70% of data center expansion by 2030
• Hyperscaler AI capex remains elevated as companies build larger rack-scale clusters

This matters because every larger AI rack requires more advanced connectivity.

More GPUs.

More bandwidth.

More switching.

More demand for companies like Astera Labs.

The biggest debate is valuation.

At nearly $500/share, investors were pricing in years of flawless AI execution.

After falling into the low $300s, sentiment has reset dramatically.

Levels I’m watching:

Support:
• $300 area
• Break below opens the possibility of $270–280

Bullish confirmation:
• Reclaim $330–350 resistance
• Potential move back toward $370–400 trendline retest

A niche AI infrastructure company, sitting in a critical bottleneck, with strong growth, high margins, and a massive secular tailwind…

down nearly 40%.

The market is deciding:

Was this an AI valuation reset…

or was the growth story actually broken?

Watching $ALAB closely.
