Pharmicell, Exclusive Supplier of Core CCL Material for NVIDIA AI Servers to Doosan
Pharmicell，向斗山供应英伟达 AI 服务器核心 CCL 材料的独家供应商

Pharmicell, whose core businesses are bio/pharmaceuticals and advanced materials, has been identified as the exclusive supplier of copper-clad laminate (CCL) raw materials for AI servers to Doosan's Electro-Materials BG (Business Group). The end customer is NVIDIA. The company's earnings have grown sharply on recent AI server demand. Once its third plant in Ulsan comes online in the second half of the year, production capacity will double.
Pharmicell 的核心业务为生物/制药及先进材料，已被指定为 Doosan 电子材料业务集团（BG）AI 服务器用覆铜板（CCL）原材料的独家供应商，其终端客户为 NVIDIA。受近期 AI 服务器需求推动，公司盈利大幅增长。一旦位于蔚山的第三工厂于下半年投产，产能将翻倍。

According to industry sources on the 10th, Pharmicell exclusively supplies low-dielectric-constant (low-Dk) electronic materials—including resin and hardener—that serve as the core raw materials for the AI server CCL produced by Doosan Electro-Materials BG. Doosan is a key partner supplying AI server CCL to global big tech companies, including NVIDIA.
据业界 10 日消息，Pharmicell 独家供应低介电常数（低 Dk）电子材料（包括树脂和硬化剂），这些材料是 Doosan 电子材料业务集团所产 AI 服务器用 CCL 的核心原材料。Doosan 是向包括 NVIDIA 在内的全球大型科技公司供应 AI 服务器用 CCL 的重要合作伙伴。

Pharmicell is reaping the benefits of being the exclusive materials supplier. Its first-quarter revenue this year came to 36.7 billion won, up 36% year-over-year. Of this, low-Dk electronic materials accounted for 26.0 billion won, or 71% of the total. Most of this is Doosan-bound revenue. That figure is more than 1.5 times the prior-year level (16.6 billion won), and roughly 7.6 times the level of Q1 2024 (3.4 billion won), when supply to Doosan began. Pharmicell's annual revenue from low-Dk electronic materials this year is projected to roughly double from last year.
Pharmicell 作为独家材料供应商正在收获回报。今年第一季度营收达到 367 亿韩元，同比增长 36%。其中，低介电常数电子材料收入为 260 亿韩元，占总营收的 71%，大部分来自斗山。该金额是去年同期（166 亿韩元）的 1.5 倍以上，约为 2024 年第一季度（34 亿韩元，当时刚向斗山供货）的 7.6 倍。预计 Pharmicell 今年低介电常数电子材料的年收入将较去年近乎翻番。

The exclusivity is also expected to continue for the time being. Because the physical properties of resin and hardener can vary depending on blending conditions and processes, a lengthy optimization process tailored to customer requirements is required. Even for products of identical composition, re-certification is needed if production equipment changes. This creates a structure in which new entrants find it difficult to break into the supply chain in the short term.
这种独家供应格局预计短期内仍将持续。由于树脂和固化剂的物理特性会因混合条件及工艺不同而发生变化，需要根据客户需求进行长时间的定制化优化。即使成分相同的产品，若生产设备变更，也需重新认证。这种结构使得新进入者在短期内难以打破供应链壁垒。

In response to rising demand, Pharmicell announced in March of last year that it would invest 30 billion won to build a third plant in Ulsan. The site is roughly 1.7 times the size of the combined footprint of the existing No. 1 and No. 2 plants. Following completion in September, it is scheduled to begin operation in Q1 2027 after going through permitting and customer re-certification procedures.
为应对日益增长的需求，Pharmicell 于去年 3 月宣布将投资 300 亿韩元在蔚山建设第三工厂。该厂区面积约为现有 1 号、2 号工厂总占地面积的 1.7 倍。工厂将于 9 月竣工，随后经过审批及客户重新认证流程，计划于 2027 年第一季度投入运营。

Once the new plant is operational, production capacity will expand to roughly double the current level. The No. 1 and No. 2 plants in Ulsan are currently running at full capacity. The plan is to produce raw materials at the existing plants and to operate the third plant as a dedicated production base for low-Dk electronic materials.
新工厂投产后，产能将扩大至目前水平的两倍左右。位于蔚山的 1 号、2 号工厂目前均已满负荷运转。现行规划是：在现有工厂生产原材料，而第三工厂则作为低介电常数电子材料的专属生产基地运营。

Pharmicell co-developed the materials with Doosan over a period of about 10 years and began supplying them in 2024. The resin and hardener that Pharmicell supplies are core materials that determine the electrical characteristics of CCL. Their low-Dk properties significantly reduce signal loss occurring during data transmission.
Pharmicell 与斗山共同研发该材料历时约十年，并于 2024 年开始供货。Pharmicell 供应的树脂与硬化剂是决定覆铜板（CCL）电气特性的核心材料，其低介电常数特性可显著降低数据传输过程中产生的信号损耗。

The resin and hardener combine with glass fiber (spun yarn) to make prepreg, the insulating layer. CCL—produced by attaching copper foil to both sides of the prepreg—is used in high-value-added substrates for AI applications. Doosan sources its glass fiber from Japan's Nittobo and its copper foil from suppliers such as Lotte Energy Materials. Pharmicell supplies resin and hardener to Doosan's largest production hub in Jeungpyeong as well as its local plant in Changshu, China.
树脂和硬化剂与玻璃纤维（纺纱）结合制成预浸料，即绝缘层。CCL——通过在预浸料两侧附上铜箔制成——用于人工智能应用的高附加值基板。斗山从日本日东纺采购玻璃纤维，从乐天能源材料等供应商采购铜箔。Pharmicell 向斗山最大的生产中心（位于增坪）及其在中国常熟的本地工厂供应树脂和硬化剂。

Pharmicell is also pursuing new customer acquisition, but for the time being it plans to focus on meeting Doosan's demand. Doosan recently announced plans to invest 180 billion won to build a new AI CCL plant in Thailand.
Pharmicell 也在寻求获取新客户，但暂时计划专注于满足斗山的需求。斗山最近宣布计划投资 1800 亿韩元，在泰国建造一座新的 AI CCL 工厂。
