## 原文

At ECTC, GUC showed a practical STCO flow for CoWoS-R style UCIe-A integration, moving from pre-layout 2D EM exploration to post-layout 3D EM extraction, transient eye simulation, PDN impedance analysis, and final power-aware SI signoff. (1/3)
在 ECTC 上，GUC 展示了针对 CoWoS-R 风格 UCIe-A 集成的实用 STCO 流程，涵盖从预布局 2D 电磁探索到后布局 3D 电磁提取、瞬态眼图仿真、PDN 阻抗分析以及最终功耗感知 SI 签收的完整过程。(1/3)  

---

 The key point is that RDL is not just “wires and polymers.” At 16 to 36 GT/s, the geometry becomes part of the channel. GUC uses GSG-interleaved transmission lines, where ground shielding traces surround the signal lines to improve isolation and help skew compensation. The
  关键在于，RDL 不仅仅是“导线和聚合物”。在 16 至 36 GT/s 的速率下，几何结构成为通道的一部分。GUC 采用 GSG 交错传输线，接地屏蔽走线环绕信号线，以改善隔离并帮助进行偏移补偿。
