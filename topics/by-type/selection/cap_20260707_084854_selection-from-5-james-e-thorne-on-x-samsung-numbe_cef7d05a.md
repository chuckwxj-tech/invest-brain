James E. Thorne
@DrJStrategy
Samsung numbers not good enough!
三星的数据还不够好！

Beyond the AI bottleneck
超越 AI 瓶颈

Artificial intelligence is not over. But  easy trade in it is.
人工智能并未终结，但轻松交易的时代已经过去。

Samsung’s latest numbers made that plain. Quarterly operating profit surged on AI‑driven memory demand and higher DRAM prices. Analysts still expect shortages into 2027. Yet the shares fell. That is what happens when a bottleneck trade gets crowded: fundamentals stay strong, but earnings stop impressing because perfection was already priced in.
三星最新财报印证了这一点。受人工智能驱动的存储需求及 DRAM 价格上涨，季度营业利润大幅攀升。分析师仍预计供应短缺将持续到 2027 年，然而股价却出现下跌。这就是瓶颈交易变得拥挤时会发生的情况：基本面依然强劲，但盈利已不再惊艳，因为完美预期早已被市场消化。

This is the fate of parabolic charts. Semiconductors, memory and AI power were the obvious chokepoints in the first phase of the AI buildout. Capital flooded in. Nvidia led. The SOX soared. Now the leaders are over‑owned and increasingly unable to turn good news into higher prices. The theme may have years left. The trade does not.
抛物线走势正是这一宿命。芯片、存储和人工智能算力作为 AI 基础设施建设第一阶段的显性瓶颈，吸引了大量资本涌入。英伟达领涨，费城半导体指数飙升。如今，领涨股已过度拥挤，即便利好频传也难以推动股价进一步上涨。主题或许仍有数年发展空间，但交易层面已然走到尽头。

The next bottlenecks are likely to be less glamorous: power, grid capacity, cooling and physical infrastructure. Markets are also beginning to price a different rates regime. Kevin Warsh’s Fed looks less Powell‑like, the Iran shock is fading and rate‑sensitive sectors are starting to stir.
接下来的瓶颈可能不那么引人注目：电力、电网容量、冷却和物理基础设施。市场也开始对不同的利率机制进行定价。凯文·沃什领导的美联储看起来不那么像鲍威尔时代，伊朗冲击正在消退，利率敏感行业开始活跃。

The lesson is simple: sell parabolic crowding and buy fear. In this market, that means trimming AI bottlenecks and looking harder at under‑owned domestic cyclicals and Bitcoin.
教训很简单：卖出抛物线式的拥挤，买入恐惧。在这个市场，这意味着削减 AI 瓶颈，更关注被低估的国内周期性股票和比特币。
