Samsung Electronics: The Last Piece in Normalizing Its Semiconductor Business Is ‘Packaging’
三星电子：半导体业务正常化的最后一块拼图是“封装”

Samsung Electronics’ semiconductor business has moved into a full rebound led by high bandwidth memory (HBM) and foundry. In advanced packaging, however, which has emerged as a core of AI chip manufacturing, the company has yet to establish a clear presence. The industry view is that securing major customers in 2.5D packaging, in particular, is urgent.
三星电子的半导体业务已全面复苏，主要得益于高带宽内存（HBM）和晶圆代工业务的强劲增长。然而，在已成为人工智能芯片制造核心的先进封装领域，该公司尚未建立起清晰的市场地位。业内普遍认为，三星电子尤其需要尽快锁定 2.5D 封装领域的主要客户。

According to industry sources on the 8th, cumulative shipments of Samsung Electronics’ in house 2.5D packaging technology ‘Cube’ are understood to still be small. The orders secured so far are also said to consist largely of startup initial volumes or short term, project based work. This stands in contrast to major competitors TSMC and Intel, which are aggressively expanding their 2.5D packaging businesses.
据业内人士 8 日透露，三星电子自主研发的 2.5D 封装技术“Cube”的累计出货量仍然很小。目前已获得的订单也主要集中在初期阶段或短期项目订单上。这与主要竞争对手台积电和英特尔形成鲜明对比，后者正在积极拓展其 2.5D 封装业务。

TSMC and Intel Surge Ahead… Samsung Electronics Lacks Major 2.5D Packaging Customers
台积电和英特尔遥遥领先……三星电子缺乏主要的 2.5D 封装客户

2.5D packaging is a technology that inserts a thin film interposer between the semiconductor chip and the substrate to boost chip performance. Its importance is growing steadily in the semiconductor industry. This is because AI accelerators, an essential element of AI data centers, are made by integrating high performance system semiconductors and HBM through 2.5D packaging. Indeed, during the period when the HBM market was expanding, Samsung Electronics wielded as a weapon a business model offering its foundry, HBM, and 2.5D packaging on a turnkey basis.
2.5D 封装技术通过在半导体芯片和基板之间插入一层薄膜中介层来提升芯片性能，其在半导体行业的重要性日益凸显。这是因为人工智能加速器（人工智能数据中心的重要组成部分）正是通过 2.5D 封装技术将高性能系统半导体和 HBM（人脑内存）集成而成。事实上，在 HBM 市场蓬勃发展的时期，三星电子凭借其代工、HBM 和 2.5D 封装一体化解决方案，在竞争中占据了主导地位。

In packaging, Samsung Electronics has worked since 2024 to expand the market for its in house 2.5D packaging technology Cube. However, there is no confirmed case yet of Samsung Electronics’ 2.5D packaging being adopted in a big tech AI accelerator. The customers currently using Samsung Electronics’ 2.5D packaging are understood to include IBM of the US and Rebellions, a domestic AI fabless startup.
在封装领域，三星电子自 2024 年以来一直致力于拓展其自主研发的 2.5D 封装技术 Cube 的市场。然而，目前尚无确凿证据表明三星电子的 2.5D 封装技术已被大型科技人工智能加速器采用。据了解，目前使用三星电子 2.5D 封装技术的客户包括美国 IBM 公司和韩国国内人工智能无晶圆厂初创公司 Rebellions。

One industry official in packaging explained, “Customers that have adopted Samsung Electronics’ 2.5D packaging platform remain at low shipment volumes or short term, project based production on a monthly cycle,” adding, “Now that we have entered an era in which advanced packaging significantly determines chip performance, Samsung Electronics also needs to concentrate on reinforcing its competitiveness in this field.”
一位包装行业的业内人士解释说：“采用三星电子 2.5D 封装平台的客户仍然保持着较低的出货量，或者以短期项目为基础，按月进行生产。”他补充道：“现在我们已经进入了一个先进封装技术显著决定芯片性能的时代，三星电子也需要集中精力加强其在该领域的竞争力。”

This contrasts with Samsung Electronics’ main competitors TSMC and Intel, each of which is achieving remarkable growth in 2.5D packaging.
这与三星电子的主要竞争对手台积电和英特尔形成了鲜明对比，这两家公司在 2.5D 封装领域都取得了显著的增长。

TSMC, which leads the global foundry market, is executing investments to expand the production capacity of its in house 2.5D packaging ‘Chip on Wafer on Substrate (CoWoS)’ from around 35,000 wafers per month at the end of last year to around 130,000 wafers per month by the end of this year.
台积电是全球晶圆代工市场的领导者，正在进行投资，以扩大其自主研发的 2.5D 封装技术“芯片封装在基板上 (CoWoS)”的产能，从去年年底的每月约 35,000 片晶圆增加到今年年底的每月约 130,000 片晶圆。

Intel is moving into full commercialization of its 2.5D packaging ‘Embedded Multi die Interconnect Bridge (EMIB).’ Google is reported to have adopted EMIB for mass production of its own AI chip, the Tensor Processing Unit (TPU), and is expected to begin mass production from next year.
英特尔正全面推进其 2.5D 封装技术“嵌入式多芯片互连桥 (EMIB)”的商业化。据报道，谷歌已采用 EMIB 技术大规模生产其人工智能芯片——张量处理单元 (TPU)，预计将于明年开始量产。

Larger Chips… Samsung Electronics Sees a Possible Turnaround with PLP
更大的芯片……三星电子认为 PLP 有望带来转机。

It is not as though Samsung Electronics has no opportunities. The company is currently shifting the development direction of its 2.5D packaging from the existing wafer level packaging (WLP) to panel level packaging (PLP).
并非三星电子没有机会。该公司目前正将其 2.5D 封装技术的发展方向从现有的晶圆级封装（WLP）转向面板级封装（PLP）。

Panel level packaging is a process that performs packaging on a wide rectangular panel. Compared with packaging on the existing wafer (300mm in diameter), it offers a larger area and allows chips to be arranged efficiently, resulting in higher productivity. In particular, with recent AI semiconductors growing in chip size to improve performance, the applicability of PLP is expected to expand.
面板级封装（PLP）是一种在宽矩形面板上进行封装的工艺。与在现有晶圆（直径 300 毫米）上进行封装相比，PLP 可提供更大的面积，并允许更高效地排列芯片，从而提高生产效率。尤其随着近年来人工智能半导体芯片尺寸的不断增大以提升性能，PLP 的应用范围预计将会进一步扩大。

Accordingly, Samsung Electronics is applying PLP instead of WLP to Cube, while also pursuing commercialization of ‘System on Panel (SoP)’ for ultra large chips. SoP is a technology that joins multiple semiconductors together on a large rectangular panel, and is currently being developed at a size of 415x510mm.
因此，三星电子在 Cube 芯片上采用的是 PLP 工艺而非 WLP 工艺，同时也在推进“系统级面板（SoP）”技术的商业化，以用于超大尺寸芯片。SoP 技术将多个半导体器件集成在一个大型矩形面板上，目前正在开发尺寸为 415x510 毫米的 SoP 芯片。

Another industry official in packaging stated, “Samsung Electronics’ neglect of mass production development for advanced packaging technology for system semiconductors, relative to memory semiconductors, risks acting as a factor that undermines its future business competitiveness,” and noted, “At the point when PLP application begins in earnest in the AI chip field, it will need to quickly secure I-Cube customers.”
另一位封装行业的业内人士表示：“三星电子忽视了系统半导体先进封装技术的量产开发，而忽视了存储半导体，这可能会削弱其未来的业务竞争力。”他还指出：“当 PLP 技术在人工智能芯片领域真正开始应用时，三星电子需要迅速锁定 I-Cube 的客户。”

Meanwhile, setting aside 2.5D packaging, Samsung Electronics’ semiconductor business is showing a clear overall improvement this year. Aggressive AI infrastructure investment by global big tech has greatly increased demand for advanced semiconductors, and the development of advanced processes has also picked up speed.
与此同时，撇开 2.5D 封装不谈，三星电子的半导体业务今年整体呈现明显改善的态势。全球大型科技公司对人工智能基础设施的大力投资大幅提升了对先进半导体的需求，先进工艺的研发也随之加速。

In February, Samsung Electronics began mass production shipments of HBM4 (the 7th generation of HBM) for NVIDIA, laying the cornerstone for a rebound in its sluggish HBM business. The company has also set a plan to expand HBM shipments by more than three times year over year this year. There are expectations that the foundry business will turn to profit as early as the second half of this year. It has recently attracted Tesla, NVIDIA, and Groq, among others, as advanced process customers.
今年 2 月，三星电子开始为英伟达量产 HBM4（第七代 HBM），为其低迷的 HBM 业务复苏奠定了基础。该公司还制定了计划，力争今年 HBM 出货量同比增长三倍以上。预计其晶圆代工业务最早将于今年下半年实现盈利。近期，该公司已吸引了特斯拉、英伟达和 Groq 等先进工艺客户。
