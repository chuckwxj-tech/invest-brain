Luxury home sales in Taipei surged in the previous quarter, with transactions exceeding NT$100 million reaching nearly 30 deals.
台北市豪宅销售在前一季度激增，成交金额超过新台币 1 亿元的案例接近 30 笔。

Fueled by the boom in Taiwan's stock market and the resulting wealth effect, luxury home sales in Taipei City skyrocketed in the second quarter. According to real estate transaction data, nearly 30 luxury home deals exceeding NT$100 million were recorded in Taipei City during Q2, including landmark projects like "Taoyuan Silver Garden" and "One Park Taipei." Among them, Taoyuan Silver Garden achieved the highest price of the year, with prices per ping (3.3 square meters) exceeding NT$3 million. This indicates that affluent individuals are gradually redirecting profits from the stock market into luxury real estate investments.
受台湾股市繁荣及由此产生的财富效应推动，台北市豪宅销售在第二季度大幅飙升。根据房地产交易数据，第二季度台北市录得近 30 笔金额超过新台币 1 亿元的豪宅交易，包括“陶园银花园”和“One Park Taipei”等地标项目。其中，“陶园银花园”创下年度最高单价，每坪（3.3 平方米）价格超过新台币 300 万元。这表明富裕群体正逐步将股市利润转向豪宅房地产投资。

Market analysts predict that the number of luxury home transactions exceeding NT$100 million in Taipei City during Q2 this year will more than double compared to the same period last year.
市场分析师预测，今年第二季度台北市金额超过新台币 1 亿元的豪宅交易数量将较去年同期翻倍以上。

Highlights of Luxury Home Transactions in Taipei City in Q2  
Tseng Ching-te, Project Manager at Sinyi Realty (9940) Real Estate Research Department, stated that after Taiwan's stock market broke through 40,000 points, the technology sector, financial industry, and asset holders benefited from the wealth effect. In particular, the sharp rise in some tech stocks significantly increased the net worth of major shareholders, leading some capital to flow into the real estate market. On one hand, they converted stock gains into tangible assets; on the other, they purchased luxury homes to meet asset allocation and personal living needs, thereby boosting transactions in the luxury home market.
台北市第二季度豪宅交易亮点
信义房屋（9940）不动产企研室专案经理曾景德表示，台股站上 4 万点后，科技业、金融业及资产持有者受惠于财富效应。尤其部分科技股涨幅剧烈，大幅提升大股东身价，使得部分资金流入房市，一方面将股票获利转为实体资产，另一方面购入豪宅满足资产配置及自用需求，因而推升豪宅市场交易。

According to real estate transaction records, among luxury homes sold for over NT$100 million in Q2, "Taoyuan Silver Garden" drew the most attention, establishing the highest per-ping price record in this year's luxury home market with transaction prices exceeding NT$3 million per ping. Additionally, "Huang Xiang Yu Ju" was sold for NT$430 million, at NT$2.129 million per ping. Two units on the 19th floor of "Taipei One Park Yuan Li Xinyi Lian Qin" in Daan District were sold for NT$399 million and NT$360 million respectively, achieving a per-ping price of about NT$2.63 million, demonstrating that Taipei City's ultra-luxury home market continues to hold strong appeal.
根据不动产交易实价登录资料显示，第二季总价超过新台币 1 亿元的豪宅交易中，最受瞩目的是「桃园银园」，以每坪单价突破 3 万元新台币的交易价格，创下今年豪宅市场最高单价纪录。此外，「黄翔玉居」以 4.3 亿元新台币成交，每坪单价约 212.9 万元新台币。大安区「台北一公园元利信义联勤」19 楼两户分别以 3.99 亿元及 3.6 亿元新台币售出，每坪单价约 263 万元新台币，显示台北市超豪宅市场仍具高度吸引力。

Lai Chih-chang, Deputy Director of the Public Relations Department at DJ Residential Planning Research Office, analyzed that while the overall housing market remains influenced by policy factors, the luxury home market is showing the opposite trend. Affluent individuals who profited from the stock market are not only focused on preserving asset value but also emphasizing property uniqueness, with a growing tendency to invest in rare luxury homes. For example, "Tao Zhu Yin Yuan" boasts landmark architectural style combined with artistic design, giving it high market recognition and making its transaction prices relatively resilient to economic fluctuations.
赖志昌，大升不动产规划研究室公关部副主任分析指出，虽然整体住宅市场仍受政策因素影响，但豪宅市场却呈现相反趋势。在股市获利的富裕族群不仅注重资产保值，更强调物件独特性，对于稀有豪宅的购入意愿日益提升。例如"陶朱隐园"兼具地标建筑风格与艺术设计，市场辨识度高，成交价格相对不受景气波动影响。

In addition to the existing home market, pre-sale luxury homes priced over NT$100 million continue to enjoy strong popularity. Publicized transactions include the June sale of a unit at "Xi Hua Pu Yuan" for NT$200 million, at NT$1.928 million per ping; two units at "Dun Mu" sold for approximately NT$190 million each, at NT$1.96 million per ping; a unit at "Zhu Yuan Nangang - Dong Yu Yuan" sold for NT$154 million, at NT$1.536 million per ping; a unit at "Wu Shuang" sold for NT$140 million, at NT$1.856 million per ping; and multiple transactions at "Song Yi" in Xinyi District, with per-ping prices exceeding NT$2 million, officially joining the "NT$2 Million Club."
除了既有主场外，总价逾亿元豪宅预售市场持续热销。据实价揭露记录，包括 6 月成交的「西华富邦」一户 2 亿元，每坪 192.8 万元；「敦睦」两户各约 1.9 亿元，每坪 196 万元；「筑亿南港·东誉苑」一户 1.54 亿元，每坪 153.6 万元；「无双」一户 1.4 亿元，每坪 185.6 万元；以及信义区「松怡」多户交易，每坪单价突破 200 万元，正式加入「200 万元俱乐部」。

Tseng Ching-te pointed out that while many luxury home transactions were concentrated in May and June, the record prices were set in July, indicating that most buyers were not affected by loan processing delays. Many of these deals are estimated to have involved large down payments or outright cash purchases. He noted that Taiwan's stock market capitalization has grown by about NT$6 trillion over the past year. Even a portion of these funds flowing into the real estate market would be sufficient to significantly boost transactions in Taipei's mid-to-high-end housing segment. The luxury home market is already reflecting this wealth effect, positioning itself as a key destination for affluent capital investment.
曾清德指出，虽然许多豪宅交易集中在 5 月和 6 月，但创纪录的价格出现在 7 月，这表明大多数买家并未受到贷款审批延迟的影响。据估计，这些交易中有相当比例涉及大额首付或全款购房。他指出，过去一年台湾股市市值增长了约新台币 6 兆元。即使其中一部分资金流入房地产市场，也足以显著提振台北中高端住宅市场的交易量。豪宅市场已开始反映这种财富效应，成为富裕资本投资的关键目标。
