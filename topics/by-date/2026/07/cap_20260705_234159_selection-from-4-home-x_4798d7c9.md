1. CXMT signed a 3-5 year long-term server DRAM supply contract with Tencent  
长鑫存储（CXMT）与腾讯签署了一份为期 3-5 年的服务器 DRAM 长期供货合同。

- Approximately $2.9 billion in scale, targeting server DRAM for cloud, database, and AI workloads.  
- 规模约 29 亿美元，瞄准面向云端、数据库和 AI 工作负载的服务器 DRAM。

- This indicates that Chinese big tech is securing domestic memory for their own AI infrastructure on a long-term basis, rather than for overseas exports.  
- 这表明中国大型科技公司正在长期为其自身 AI 基础设施锁定国产内存，而非用于海外出口。

2. The backdrop to Chinese companies increasing their use of domestic memory is the global memory shortage.  
2. 中国公司增加使用国产内存的背景，是全球内存短缺。

- Amid the global memory shortage, Chinese companies are expanding their use of domestic memory. 
- 在全球内存短缺的背景下，中国公司正在扩大对国产内存的使用。

- Analysis suggests that rather than CXMT's supply being left over for export, there's a strong incentive for it to be absorbed within China.  
- 分析表明，长鑫存储的供应并非用于出口剩余，而是有强烈动机在中国国内消化。

3. YMTC is also benefiting from domestic demand and supply shortages, as well as rising prices in China.  
3. 长江存储同样受益于国内需求与供应短缺，以及中国市场的价格上涨。

- Counterpoint analyzes that YMTC saw significant growth in the NAND market in Q1 2026, driven by strong demand from Chinese domestic manufacturers and price increases due to supply shortages.  
- Counterpoint 分析指出，受中国本土制造商的强劲需求及供应短缺推动的价格上涨，长江存储 2026 年第一季度 NAND 市场实现显著增长。

4. CXMT remains limited on the HBM front.  
4. 长鑫存储在高带宽存储器方面仍然受限。

- SemiAnalysis expects CXMT to increase HBM wafer capacity in response to growth in China's domestic compute market,  
but estimates it at 55k wspm in 2027 and 100k wspm in 2028. 
- SemiAnalysis 预计 CXMT 将增加 HBM 晶圆产能，以应对中国本土计算市场的增长，
但估计 2027 年该产能为 5.5 万片/月，2028 年达到 10 万片/月。

- It's not yet positioned to fully meet China's overall AI demand right away, but rather in the process of belatedly allocating capacity to align with domestic AI needs.  
- 目前尚未能立即完全满足中国整体 AI 需求，而是正在滞后地分配产能，以与国内 AI 需求对齐。
