Samsung Nears Over KRW 10 Trillion in Semiconductor Equipment Orders for Pyeongtaek Plant 5
三星平泽 P5 工厂半导体设备订单额即将突破 10 万亿韩元

Samsung Electronics is beginning its investment in semiconductor equipment for Pyeongtaek Plant 5 (P5). The company is expected to finalize its selection of semiconductor equipment suppliers for the first production line shortly and is reportedly moving forward simultaneously with equipment procurement for the second line. Expectations are rising across the industry as equipment orders worth well over KRW 10 trillion appear imminent.
三星电子开始对平泽五厂（P5）进行半导体设备投资。该公司预计将很快敲定第一条生产线的半导体设备供应商选择，并据悉正同步推进第二条生产线的设备采购。随着规模远超 10 万亿韩元的设备订单即将到来，业界期待值持续攀升。

According to industry sources on the 15th, Samsung Electronics’ Device Solutions division has entered the final stage of selecting semiconductor manufacturing equipment for Phase 1, P5’s first production line. The fab’s structural framework is currently being erected, while facilities for supplying specialty gases and chemicals used in semiconductor manufacturing are also under construction. Purchase orders are expected to begin once equipment selection for P5 Phase 1 is completed.
据业界 15 日消息，三星电子 Device Solutions 部门已进入 P5 一期第一条生产线半导体制造设备的最终选型阶段。该晶圆厂的结构框架正在搭建中，同时用于半导体制造的特种气体和化学品的供应设施也在建设中。预计 P5 一期设备选型完成后将开始下达采购订单。

“Most of the equipment suppliers for the P5 Phase 1 line have already been decided, while the selection process for suppliers to Phase 2 and subsequent lines is proceeding at the same time,” said an industry source familiar with the matter. “Equipment orders for P5 are about to begin in earnest.”
"P5 一期生产线的多数设备供应商已经确定，而二期及后续生产线的供应商遴选工作也在同步推进。"一位知情行业人士表示，"P5 的设备订购即将全面展开。"

P5 is planned as a massive three-story fab containing six cleanrooms. It will be larger than Samsung Electronics’ other fabs at its Pyeongtaek campus, which have two-story “double-fab” structures. Samsung aims to increase P5’s production capacity by more than 50% compared with its existing fabs. The plant is expected to manufacture cutting-edge DRAM products, including high-bandwidth memory (HBM). Samsung is also reportedly considering adding a semiconductor foundry line, depending on market demand.
P5 规划为一座配备六个洁净室的三层巨型晶圆厂，规模将超过三星电子平泽园区内采用"双工厂"双层结构的其他工厂。三星力求将 P5 的产能较现有工厂提升 50%以上。该工厂预计将生产包括高带宽存储器（HBM）在内的尖端 DRAM 产品。据称三星还在考虑根据市场需求增设半导体代工生产线。

Samsung Electronics has also begun selecting equipment suppliers for Phase 2, P5’s second line. Semiconductor fabs are generally developed sequentially in sections—such as Phase 1, Phase 2 and Phase 3—based on each line’s intended purpose and operating schedule. The move suggests Samsung plans to bring equipment into Phase 2 quickly and accelerate the start of operations.
三星电子已着手为 P5 二期生产线遴选设备供应商。半导体工厂通常按照各条生产线的功能定位与运营时间表，以一期、二期、三期等阶段性方式依次开发。此举表明三星计划快速向二期厂房引入设备，从而加快投产进程。

P5 was originally expected to become fully operational in 2028, but the first line, Phase 1, could begin operating as early as next year. The key variable will be equipment delivery lead times following the placement of orders. Samsung has asked its equipment partners to bring forward their delivery schedules as much as possible.
P5 原计划于 2028 年全面投产，但第一阶段产线最早可能于明年开始运营。关键变量将取决于订单下达后的设备交付周期。三星已要求其设备合作伙伴尽可能提前交付计划。

These moves are intended to enable Samsung to respond swiftly to conditions in the memory market. The global shortage of memory chips is intensifying as investment in artificial intelligence infrastructure expands. Samsung is therefore seeking to increase production capacity rapidly to meet surging demand. The strategy is also aimed at expanding its memory supply capabilities ahead of competitors and securing market leadership.
这些举措旨在使三星能够迅速应对存储芯片市场的变化。随着人工智能基础设施投资持续扩大，全球存储芯片短缺问题日益加剧。因此，三星正寻求快速提升产能以满足激增的需求。该策略还旨在赶超竞争对手扩大存储芯片供应能力，巩固市场领导地位。

Although Samsung Electronics is planning semiconductor fab investments in Yongin, Gyeonggi Province, and Gwangju, South Jeolla Province, its Pyeongtaek complex is currently the only location where it can immediately add large-scale production capacity in response to market conditions.
尽管三星电子计划在京畿道龙仁市和全罗南道光州市投资建设半导体晶圆厂，但目前只有平泽园区能够立即根据市场状况增加大规模产能。

The semiconductor equipment industry is also paying close attention. Equipment purchases typically account for 70–80% of the cost of building a semiconductor fab, meaning P5’s equipment orders are expected to generate substantial benefits for suppliers.
半导体设备行业也在密切关注。设备采购通常占半导体晶圆厂建设成本的 70%至 80%，这意味着 P5 的设备订单预计将为供应商带来可观的收益。

“Samsung Electronics is contacting global and Korean equipment suppliers simultaneously as it prepares for large-scale equipment purchases,” said a semiconductor equipment industry official. “Given that P5 will be a state-of-the-art fab, supply opportunities will go to equipment makers that have secured a competitive edge through differentiated performance.”
