Reuters: Intel and AMD are pursuing long-term server CPU supply agreements with major Chinese server customers in response to surging prices and tightening supply.

The proposed agreements, ranging from one to more than two years, would primarily guarantee purchase volumes while allowing prices to adjust in line with market conditions. In addition to one-year contracts, discussions are also underway for agreements extending beyond two years.

In China’s market, prices for some server CPUs have risen more than 10% month over month, with certain products up over 40% since the beginning of the year.

Lead times for some of Intel’s server CPU products have stretched to as long as six months, underscoring the growing supply-demand imbalance.
路透社：英特尔和 AMD 正寻求与中国主要服务器客户达成长期服务器 CPU 供应协议，以应对价格飙升和供应紧张的局面。

拟议的协议期限从一年到两年以上不等，主要将保障采购量，同时允许价格根据市场状况进行调整。除了一年期的合同外，双方也在商讨为期两年以上的协议。

在中国市场，部分服务器 CPU 的价格月环比上涨超过 10%，自年初以来某些产品的涨幅已超过 40%。

英特尔部分服务器 CPU 产品的交货周期已延长至长达六个月，凸显出日益加剧的供需失衡。
