The Information: ASML Plans Price Increases for Chipmaking Equipment, Despite TSMC Resistance
消息：尽管台积电反对，ASML 仍计划提高芯片制造设备价格

Article Summary:
文章摘要：

- ASML is considering raising the prices of its EUV systems, citing surging demand from the AI and memory semiconductor markets. It has also notified some customers that it plans to increase DUV equipment prices by 10%.
- TSMC, ASML’s largest customer, is pushing back against price increases for both EUV and DUV systems. However, some Chinese semiconductor companies have accepted the higher DUV prices.
- ASML has raised its 2026 revenue outlook to €43 billion–€45 billion and plans to increase annual production capacity for two of its key systems by 30% this year.
- TSMC faces a dilemma: ASML’s equipment is essential for expanding its AI chip and advanced CoWoS packaging capacity, but the company also wants to keep costs under control while securing additional systems.
- China is likely to be hit hardest by the DUV price increases because export restrictions prevent Chinese companies from purchasing EUV systems and certain advanced DUV systems, while alternative suppliers remain limited.
- ASML 正考虑提高其 EUV 系统的价格，理由是人工智能和存储半导体市场需求激增。该公司还已通知部分客户，计划将 DUV 设备价格提高 10%。
- ASML 的最大客户台积电正在反对 EUV 和 DUV 系统的涨价。然而，一些中国半导体公司已接受了更高的 DUV 价格。
ASML 将其 2026 年营收预期上调至 430 亿至 450 亿欧元，并计划今年将两大关键系统的年产能提高 30%。
台积电面临两难困境：ASML 的设备对其扩大 AI 芯片和先进 CoWoS 封装产能至关重要，但该公司也希望在确保额外系统的同时控制成本。
中国可能会受到 DUV 价格上涨的最大冲击，因为出口限制阻止中国公司购买 EUV 系统和某些先进 DUV 系统，而替代供应商仍然有限。

Jukan’s Take:
Jukan 的观点：

TEL and other equipment makers are already pursuing aggressive price increases. In meetings with the media and sell-side analysts, TEL said it is seeking to raise equipment prices by as much as 50% over the long term.
TEL 及其他设备制造商正在积极推动大幅涨价。在与媒体和卖方分析师的会议上，TEL 表示，其长期目标是寻求将设备价格提高多达 50%。

Lead times across the semiconductor equipment industry have already increased significantly. Given the circumstances, it is only natural that toolmakers would want to raise prices. Still, it is somewhat surprising that TSMC is already pushing back. TSMC appears to be extremely focused on protecting its margins.
半导体设备行业的交货周期已显著延长。在这种情况下，设备制造商自然希望提高价格。尽管如此，台积电已经开始抵制仍然有些令人意外。台积电似乎极为注重保护其利润率。

The Information: Apple Hunts for AI Chip Acquisitions
The Information：苹果寻求 AI 芯片收购机会

- Apple is actively searching for acquisition targets, including semiconductor startups, to strengthen its in-house AI server chip capabilities.
- Its current M2 Ultra-based servers have limitations when processing large AI models, forcing Apple to run some features of the new Siri on Nvidia chips hosted in Google Cloud.
- Delays to Apple’s next-generation AI server chip, “Baltra,” have also fueled concerns that the company’s semiconductor design expertise remains too heavily focused on mobile applications.
- Apple is seeking to reduce its dependence on Nvidia through M5 and M7 Ultra-based server chips and a joint development project with Broadcom. However, the M7 Ultra-based product is not expected until 2029.
- Given Apple’s recent consideration of major acquisitions, potential changes to its cash policy, and an eventual CEO transition, the company may be more willing than in the past to pursue a large-scale acquisition of a semiconductor or AI company.
- 苹果正在积极寻找收购目标，包括半导体初创公司，以增强其内部 AI 服务器芯片能力。
- 其当前基于 M2 Ultra 的服务器在处理大型 AI 模型时存在局限，迫使苹果在谷歌云托管的英伟达芯片上运行新版 Siri 的部分功能。
- 苹果下一代 AI 服务器芯片“Baltra”的延迟也加剧了担忧，即该公司在半导体设计方面的专长仍过于侧重于移动应用。
- 苹果正试图通过基于 M5 和 M7 Ultra 的服务器芯片，以及与博通合作开发的联合项目，来减少对英伟达的依赖。然而，基于 M7 Ultra 的产品预计要到 2029 年才能问世。
- 鉴于苹果近期对重大收购的考量、现金政策的可能调整以及最终的首席执行官更迭，该公司可能比以往更愿意对半导体或 AI 公司进行大规模收购。

Jukan’s Take:
Jukan's Take:

The delay to Baltra, combined with Apple’s search for semiconductor startup acquisitions, raises the question of whether Apple is effectively admitting that it lacks sufficient expertise in server chips.
Baltra 的延迟，加上苹果寻求收购半导体初创公司，引发了一个问题：苹果是否在实际上承认自己在服务器芯片领域缺乏足够的技术专长？

ETNews: Samsung’s Pyeongtaek Plant 5 Poised to Place More Than KRW 10 Trillion in Semiconductor Equipment Orders
ETNews：三星平泽 P5 工厂有望下达超过 10 万亿韩元的半导体设备订单

- Samsung Electronics has nearly completed the selection of equipment suppliers for Phase 1, the first production line at its Pyeongtaek Plant 5 (P5), and is expected to begin issuing purchase orders shortly. Supplier selection for Phase 2 is also underway, meaning the total order value is expected to significantly exceed KRW 10 trillion.
- 三星电子已基本完成平泽 P5 工厂第一条生产线 Phase 1 的设备供应商筛选，预计将很快开始下达采购订单。Phase 2 的供应商筛选工作也在进行中，这意味着订单总额预计将远超 10 万亿韩元。

- P5 will be a massive fab featuring six cleanrooms across three floors, with a target of increasing production capacity by more than 50% compared with Samsung’s existing Pyeongtaek fabs.
- Its main products will be advanced DRAM, including HBM. Samsung is also considering adding foundry lines depending on market conditions.
- The entire facility is scheduled to become operational by 2028, but Phase 1 could begin operations as early as next year if equipment deliveries proceed smoothly.
- To address the global memory shortage and expanding AI infrastructure demand, Samsung has asked equipment suppliers to shorten their delivery lead times.
- Because equipment purchases account for approximately 70%–80% of total fab construction costs, domestic and international semiconductor equipment companies with strong technological capabilities could be major beneficiaries.
- P5 将是一座巨型晶圆厂，设有三层共六个洁净室，目标是相比三星现有的平泽工厂产能提升超过 50%。
- 其主要产品将是先进 DRAM，包括 HBM。三星还考虑根据市场情况增设代工线。
- 整个设施计划于 2028 年投入运营，但若设备交付顺利，第一阶段最早可在明年开始运营。
- 为应对全球内存短缺及日益增长的 AI 基础设施需求，三星已要求设备供应商缩短交货周期。
- 由于设备采购约占晶圆厂建设总成本的 70%–80%，具备强大技术实力的国内外半导体设备公司可能成为主要受益者。
