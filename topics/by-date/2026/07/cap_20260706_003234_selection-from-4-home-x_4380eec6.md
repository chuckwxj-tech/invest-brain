Foxconn’s 2nd quarter revenue soared 39.8% year-on-year to NT$2.51 trillion (US$78.69 billion) due to strong global demand for AI servers and networking products, media report.

In the 3rd quarter, Foxconn expects momentum to continue to grow versus Q2 and year-on-year, as “…AI rack shipments are expected to maintain growth, and operations will gradually gain momentum as ICT products enter peak season.”

June revenue rose 52.1% to NT$821.8 billion

January to June revenue rose 34.99% to NT$4.64 trillion
据媒体报道，受全球对 AI 服务器和网络产品的强劲需求推动，富士康第二季度营收同比增长 39.8%，达到 2.51 万亿新台币（约 786.9 亿美元）。

富士康预计，第三季度营收将较第二季度和去年同期继续增长，因为“……AI 机架出货量预计将保持增长，随着 ICT 产品进入旺季，运营将逐步获得动力。”

6 月营收增长 52.1%，达到 8218 亿新台币。

1 月至 6 月营收增长 34.99%，达到 4.64 万亿新台币。
