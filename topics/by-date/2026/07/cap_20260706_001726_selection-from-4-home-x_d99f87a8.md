## 原文

>> Global semiconductor prices are rising across all categories, with AI-use MLCC prices surging by up to 10x.

• Starting July 1, more than 20 companies across the global semiconductor industry began raising prices across all product categories. In particular, some AI-use MLCC specifications have seen prices jump by 3–10x. Distributors in China’s Huaqiangbei said, “Quotes can change within half a day, and in some cases prices rise 50% within just two hours.” As a result, distributors that have been building inventory are increasingly concerned they could end up holding stock bought near the peak.

• Overseas, companies including Infineon have raised prices by 10–25%, while domestic Chinese product prices have also risen by more than 15%. Infineon expects revenue from its AI power-solution business to reach €1.5 billion.

• Meanwhile, continued reductions in 8-inch wafer capacity are worsening supply shortages, supporting price increases across semiconductor components more broadly.
>> 全球半导体价格全线攀升，AI 用 MLCC 价格暴涨最高达 10 倍。

• 自 7 月 1 日起，全球半导体行业超过 20 家企业开启全品类涨价。其中部分 AI 用 MLCC 规格价格暴涨 3 至 10 倍。深圳华强北分销商表示："报价半天一变，部分产品两小时内涨幅达 50%。"因此，此前囤货的分销商越发担心可能在高位接盘。

• 海外方面，英飞凌等企业涨幅达 10%至 25%，国内产品价格也上涨超过 15%。英飞凌预计其 AI 电源解决方案业务收入将达到 15 亿欧元。

• 与此同时，8 英寸晶圆产能持续缩减加剧供应短缺，进一步支撑半导体元器件全面涨价。
