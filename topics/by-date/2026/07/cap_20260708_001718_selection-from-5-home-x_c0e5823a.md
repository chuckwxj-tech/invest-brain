In our current capacity-limited environment, the last thing the industry needs is lower bit output per fab from lower stacking yield. Both Kioxia and Samsung unveiled multi-array hybrid bonded NAND architectures at VLSI 2026 as a path to 1000+ layers for maximum density. Kioxia's Multi-Stacked cell Array (MSA) samples include a dual-218L (2-deck) stack to address mechanical integration challenges from alignment and warpage, and a dual-17L stack for electrical characterization and QLC reliability. Samsung's Cell Multi-Bonding (CMB) samples went further, with a dual-450L (3-deck) mechanical sample and a dual-155L (1-deck) electrical sample. (1/2)
在当前产能受限的环境中，行业最不需要的就是因堆叠良率降低而导致每座晶圆厂的位元产出下降。铠侠和三星均在 VLSI 2026 上展示了多阵列混合键合 NAND 架构，作为迈向 1000+层以实现最高密度的路径。铠侠的多堆叠单元阵列（MSA）样品包括双 218 层（2 层堆叠）架构，以解决对准和翘曲带来的机械集成挑战，以及双 17 层堆叠架构用于电气特性表征和 QLC 可靠性。三星的单元多键合（CMB）样品更进一步，推出了双 450 层（3 层堆叠）机械样品和双 155 层（1 层堆叠）电气样品。（1/2）
