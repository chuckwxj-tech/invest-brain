Chip Shortages  
Analog ICs, PCIe Retimers, etc.
芯片短缺
模拟 IC、PCIe 重定时器等

(Introduction) Extended chip delivery times have become the new norm, with the cloud AI capacity race and price increases impacting Taiwan's supply chain.
（引言）芯片交期延长已成新常态，云端 AI 算力竞赛与涨价冲击台湾供应链。

Reports of gradually lengthening chip delivery times have been increasing recently. According to publicly available information, analog chip maker Analog Devices (ADI) has notified customers that delivery times for some analog chip products have been extended to six months due to supply pressures from rising demand, and is advising them to place orders early to avoid delays.
近期芯片交期逐渐拉长的消息日益增多。根据公开信息，模拟芯片制造商亚德诺半导体（ADI）已通知客户，由于需求增长带来的供应压力，部分模拟芯片产品的交期已延长至六个月，并建议客户尽早下单以避免延误。

Another sales agent pointed out that due to STMicroelectronics (STM) microcontroller (MCU) delivery times being extended to 52 weeks, the agent has begun inquiring with customers about their full-year order needs for 2027.
另一位代理商指出，由于意法半导体（STMicroelectronics）微控制器（MCU）交期延长至 52 周，该代理商已开始向客户询价 2027 年全年订单需求。

On the pricing front, major manufacturers in Europe and the US are also frequently raising prices. Texas Instruments (TI) has announced a price increase for core products such as power management ICs (PMICs) and MOSFETs effective July 1, marking the third such increase in a year. STMicroelectronics implemented its second price hike targeting 2026 on June 28, and European manufacturers like Infineon and NXP have followed suit, initiating their second round of price adjustments.
在价格方面，欧美主要厂商也在频繁涨价。德州仪器（TI）宣布自 7 月 1 日起对电源管理 IC（PMIC）和 MOSFET 等核心产品进行提价，这已是其一年内的第三次涨价。意法半导体（STMicroelectronics）于 6 月 28 日实施了针对 2026 年的第二次涨价，而英飞凌（Infineon）和恩智浦（NXP）等欧洲厂商也跟进，启动了第二轮价格调整。

Looking at the factors behind this prolonged chip delivery time trend, both are related to supply shortages, though the underlying reasons differ somewhat.
审视这一轮芯片交期延长的背后因素，两者都与供应短缺有关，但具体原因略有不同。

For mature manufacturing processes, Texas Instruments is in the midst of transitioning from 8-inch to 12-inch production lines, with delivery times generally around 12–20 weeks. Power semiconductors are generally struggling to keep up with demand for AI server power supplies, and delivery times for power products from some integrated device manufacturers (IDMs) have reached 30 weeks. Infineon specifically named AI data centers in its price increase notice, citing them as the cause of shortages in power switches and ICs.
在成熟制程方面，德州仪器正处于从 8 英寸向 12 英寸产线切换的阶段，交期普遍在 12–20 周左右。功率半导体整体难以满足 AI 服务器电源的需求，部分集成器件制造商（IDM）的功率产品交期已达 30 周。英飞凌在其涨价通知中特别点名 AI 数据中心，称其导致功率开关和 IC 短缺。

Meanwhile, competitive pressures from the adoption of advanced process technologies are also starting to emerge. In the field of high-speed transmission interfaces, reports indicate that delivery times for PCIe Gen5 retimers from major US manufacturers have reached 52 weeks. This is mainly because these retimers are gradually shifting to 7nm and 6nm processes, sharing production capacity with many AI accelerator chips and wireless communication-related chips, leading to lower priority in tight allocation quotas.
与此同时，采用先进制程技术带来的竞争压力也开始显现。在高速传输接口领域，有报告指出，美国主要厂商的 PCIe Gen5 重定时器交付时间已延长至 52 周。这主要是因为这类重定时器正逐步转向 7nm 和 6nm 制程，与众多 AI 加速芯片及无线通信相关芯片共享产能，在紧张的分配配额中优先级较低。

Furthermore, the head of Broadcom's physical layer products division publicly warned that TSMC's production capacity would peak in 2026 and supply would halt by the end of March 2026. This warning is not expected to be lifted until phased capacity expansions begin in 2027.
此外，博通物理层产品部门负责人公开警告称，台积电的产能将在 2026 年达到顶峰，并于 2026 年 3 月底停止供货。预计要到 2027 年开始分阶段扩产后，这一警告才会解除。

This wave of delivery delays is affecting not only major manufacturers in Europe and the US or the cloud AI sector, but also Taiwan's IC design companies, which generally report that delivery times have recently lengthened even in application areas outside cloud AI.
这一波交货延迟不仅影响欧美大厂或云端 AI 领域，台湾的 IC 设计公司也普遍反映，即使在云端 AI 之外的应用领域，最近交货时间也有所拉长。

Taiwan's analog chip makers believe this could become the new norm for the foreseeable future.
台湾模拟芯片厂商认为，这可能会成为可预见未来的新常态。

As mature processes and packaging capacity are allocated to AI-related demand, following the order of wafer and packaging capacity distribution, the shortages are spilling over into general applications such as consumer products and industrial controls. While end-user demand in these areas has not shown significant increases at present, product availability has become substantially more difficult.
随着成熟制程和封装产能被分配给 AI 相关需求，按照晶圆和封装产能分配的优先顺序，短缺正蔓延至消费电子和工业控制等通用应用领域。尽管目前这些领域的终端用户需求并未显著增长，但产品供应已变得相当困难。

However, the company also emphasized that the current supply shortages and prolonged delivery times are the main factors driving price increases, following the lead of major manufacturers.
然而，该公司也强调，当前供应短缺和交货期延长是推动价格上涨的主要因素，这紧随主要制造商的步伐。

The delivery time extensions and price hikes by major manufacturers are widely seen as easing the pricing pressures that Taiwan's analog chip makers have faced over the past two years. This will likely benefit companies in areas such as PMICs, motor driver ICs, USB PD controller ICs, server power supplies, and industrial control applications.
主要制造商延长交货期和提价被广泛视为缓解了台湾模拟芯片制造商过去两年面临的定价压力。这将可能惠及 PMIC、电机驱动 IC、USB PD 控制器 IC、服务器电源和工业控制应用等领域的公司。

Moreover, to mitigate risks from reliance on single suppliers, customers will accelerate certification of secondary suppliers. Companies that complete the certification process are expected to take on emergency or transfer orders, presenting new opportunities for business expansion.
此外，为降低对单一供应商的依赖风险，客户将加速认证二级供应商。预计完成认证流程的企业将承担紧急订单或转单，从而为企业扩张带来新机遇。

However, Taiwanese companies must also wait in line to secure their own wafer fabrication and packaging capacity. Whether order transfers can translate into actual shipments will depend entirely on the amount of production capacity allocated to each firm. This situation will also motivate Taiwan's IC design firms to join the "capacity scramble."
然而，台湾公司也需排队争取自身的晶圆代工和封装产能。订单转移能否转化为实际出货量，完全取决于每家厂商获得的产能分配。这一状况也将促使台湾 IC 设计公司加入“产能争夺战”。

Many industry insiders acknowledge that prices for wafer fabrication, packaging, and testing will continue to rise, but emphasize that to enhance business growth and corporate development potential, securing as much capacity as possible—even at higher costs—is essential. They have not ruled out the possibility of offering high deposits or long-term contracts again if necessary.
许多业界人士承认，晶圆代工、封装及测试价格将持续上涨，但强调为了提升业务增长与企业发展潜力，必须尽可能获取更多产能——即使成本更高也在所不惜。他们不排除在必要时再次提供高额定金或签订长期合同的可能性。
