Good morning! 6/12 Foreign Media Summary
早上好！6 月 12 日外媒摘要

- 4958 Zhen Ding: US Firm Raises Target
The US investment bank's global team has significantly raised its shipment forecasts for optical transceivers in 2026-2028; from 53 / 71 / 80mn upgraded to 73 / 141 / 15mn. Among them, the volume for 1.6T is 29 / 79 / 87mn, and in the year after next, 3.2T will reach 8mn.
- 4958 臻鼎：美国机构上调目标价
美国投资银行全球团队大幅上调了 2026-2028 年光模块出货量预测；从 5300 万/7100 万/8000 万个上调至 7300 万/1.41 亿/1500 万个。其中，1.6T 的出货量为 2900 万/7900 万/8700 万个，而后年 3.2T 将达到 800 万个。

Brokers estimate the CAGR for the optical transceiver PCB market from 25-28 at 83%. As we move toward higher-speed transmission, mSAP process capability is especially crucial—it's the ideal technology for optical transceivers. Brokers see capable manufacturers as 4958 Zhen Ding, 2313 Hwa Tong, and 3037 Shinko.
券商估计，25-28 年间光模块 PCB 市场的复合年增长率为 83%。随着向更高速度传输发展，mSAP 工艺能力尤为关键——它是光模块的理想技术。券商认为具备该能力的厂商包括 4958 臻鼎、2313 华通和 3037 欣兴。

Brokers particularly view Zhen Ding as the primary beneficiary. Additionally, in the ABF space, it's expanding customers, including Google TPU. Brokers have raised 26-28 EPS to $14.5/$24.2/$33.4, valuing it at 27.5x PE for next year, and accordingly raising the target.
券商尤其将臻鼎视为主要受益者。此外，在 ABF 领域，它正在拓展客户，包括谷歌 TPU。券商已将 2026-2028 年每股收益上调至 14.5 美元/24.2 美元/33.4 美元，基于明年 27.5 倍市盈率估值，并相应上调目标价。

- 3037 Shinko: US Firm Raises Target
The US investment bank, building on the above, observes that Shinko is one of the suppliers benefiting from growing optical transceiver demand. Separately, it expects ABF to return to supply shortages starting next year, but with a 5-10% supply gap still in 2030. T-glass supply shortages will be a key point to watch, so it believes suppliers will prioritize high-end products. Raises 26-28 EPS to $11.8/$25.3/$51, valuing at 51x PE for next year, and accordingly raising the target.
- 3037 新光：美国公司上调目标价
美国投行在此基础上指出，新光是受益于光收发器需求增长的供应商之一。另外，该投行预计 ABF 基板将从明年起重新出现供应短缺，但到 2030 年仍存在 5-10%的供应缺口。T-glass 供应短缺将成为关键观察点，因此认为供应商将优先供应高端产品。将 26-28 年每股收益预期上调至 11.8/25.3/51 美元，按明年 51 倍市盈率估值，并据此上调目标价。
