## 原文

UMC is entering next-generation glass packaging technology.

Beyond its silicon interposer efforts, it is investing in TGV (through-glass via) processes for advanced packaging. 

Focus areas include post-TGV integration steps like front-side RDL, temporary bonding, grinding, CMP, and glass carrier handling.

Glass substrates offer advantages over silicon interposers, lower signal loss, high flatness, and dimensional stability, making them suitable for high-frequency, high-speed, and large-size AI/HPC packaging with GPUs and HBM.

This positions UMC to capture more AI-related advanced packaging opportunities and expand its specialty process business.
