In AI accelerators, switches are typically placed between compute units and memory. Etched's first patent application is a document that aims to eliminate those switches.
在 AI 加速器中，交换机通常放置在计算单元和内存之间。Etched 的第一份专利申请文件旨在消除这些交换机。

US20240378175A1, filed on May 10, 2023, claims a structure that directly connects memory channels to rows of a massive multi-chip compute array without any switching elements, with co-founders Gavin Uberti and Christopher Zhu listed as inventors.
US20240378175A1，申请于 2023 年 5 月 10 日，主张了一种将内存通道直接连接至大规模多芯片计算阵列行且无需任何交换元件的结构，发明人包括联合创始人 Gavin Uberti 与 Christopher Zhu。

Three years later, in the stealth-ending thread, Etched's touted memory philosophy "the best layer is no layer" is therefore not marketing—it's the language of the claims from the May 2023 filing.
三年后，在终结隐身状态的帖子中，Etched 所推崇的内存理念——“最佳层即无层”——因此并非营销话术，而是源自 2023 年 5 月那份专利申请的权利要求语言。

The structure can be summarized in a single diagram. HBM channels cannot communicate with each other, so the industry has inserted switches or crossbars between memory and compute. This application hardwires each channel to its own row in the array for workloads where weights don't move, eliminating that layer altogether. The advantages noted in the specification are savings in space and power.
该结构可用一张图表概括。由于 HBM 通道之间无法相互通信，业界通常会在内存与计算之间插入交换机或交叉开关。而此申请将每个通道硬连线至阵列中对应的行，适用于权重无需移动的工作负载，从而完全去除了那一层。说明书中提及的优势包括节省空间与功耗。

The article lays out how this idea existed as a document three years ahead of the company's pitch, and where it stands now at the patent office threshold. 
文章阐述了这一构思如何比公司公开宣讲早了三年以文件形式存在，以及如今其在专利局审批阶段的状况。
