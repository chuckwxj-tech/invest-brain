$VIVO is becoming one of the most interesting AI-infrastructure speculation setups on the market right now.

This is NOT confirmed. But the dot-connection is getting hard to ignore.

Here’s the full thesis:

VivoPower already announced it selected a preferred “global AI industry leader” as tenant for its 41.5MW operational Mo i Rana, Norway AI data center.

The company said identity + material terms are expected in the near term, subject to final legal docs.

That alone is a real catalyst.

Now the interesting part:

$CBRS / Cerebras recently announced a major European AI compute expansion targeting 200MW by end of 2027, specifically mentioning Norway + Finland + France.

That matters because $VIVO has:

Norway now: 41.5MW operational Mo i Rana site
Expansion potential: another ~40MW possible
Finland angle: powered-land pipeline / AI data center footprint
Multi-jurisdiction language: VIVO said discussions expanded beyond Mo i Rana

That is the first major dot.

Then comes the wording.

Cerebras-related posts/screenshots showed stronger language around “signed 200MW”, then wording appeared softened to “plans / contracting / coming online.”

That could mean nothing.

Or it could mean legal/IR cleaned it up because specific site/operator agreements are not fully announceable yet.

Interesting timing.

Then the flight dot:

A NetJets Europe flight reportedly went from Paris Le Bourget → Bodø, Norway shortly after Cerebras’ Paris/Raise Summit activity.

Bodø is a logical access point to Mo i Rana.

At the same time, Kevin Chin / VIVO-related posts allegedly showed activity around Mo i Rana.

Does that prove Cerebras was on the plane? No.

But if you’re building a theory around final site diligence / legal execution / partner announcement timing, the route is very interesting.

Now add the market structure.

$VIVO reportedly has:

Tiny float: around 2.4M–2.7M shares
Reported short interest: over 3M shares
Reported short float: 120%–140%+ depending on source
Shares on loan elevated
Options loaded at $5 / $7.50 / $10
Stock just reclaimed and closed above $5

That is real squeeze fuel.

If the tenant is just some random name, maybe this is a normal PR move.

But if the tenant is Cerebras, and the announcement includes a signed lease + material commercial terms, this becomes a completely different trade.

The DGXX comparison is why traders care.

Cerebras already signed a separate ~40MW AI data center capacity deal with DGXX, reportedly valued around $1.1B over 10 years, with potential upside through extensions.

VIVO’s Mo i Rana site is 41.5MW.

Almost the same size.

If VIVO lands anything remotely comparable, the current valuation could look wildly mispriced.

The real bull case:

Cerebras is the unnamed global AI leader.
VIVO is part of the Norway/Finland 200MW Europe buildout.
Mo i Rana is the first piece.
Finland/multi-jurisdiction pipeline becomes the bigger story.
Shorts are trapped in a tiny float.

If that’s right, this is not just a tenant PR.

It is an AI-infrastructure re-rate + short squeeze setup.

The levels are clear:

$5 = reclaimed
$5.50–$6 = momentum confirmation
$7.50 = gamma/short pain zone
$10 = full squeeze territory
$12–$16 = realistic strong Cerebras announcement zone
$18–$22+ = possible blowoff if terms are huge

Again: not confirmed.

No official PR says Cerebras + VivoPower yet.

But the overlap is real:

VIVO: Norway + Finland + unnamed global AI leader + near-term terms
CBRS: Norway + Finland + 200MW Europe AI compute + more capacity coming

Watching closely.
