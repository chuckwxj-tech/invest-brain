## 原文

Morgan Stanley: CPO Update
摩根士丹利：CPO 更新

> Rapid Scale-Out CPO Growth: CPO switch shipments are expected to grow from 23k units in 2026 (mostly 100T switches led by NVIDIA's Spectrum) to 59k in 2027, and reach 200k units by 2030, representing a 144% CAGR from 2024 to 2030.
> 快速横向扩展的 CPO 增长：CPO 交换机出货量预计将从 2026 年的 23,000 台（主要由英伟达 Spectrum 驱动的 100T 交换机）增长至 2027 年的 59,000 台，到 2030 年达到 200,000 台，2024 至 2030 年复合年增长率达 144%。

> Coupling Mainstream: Grating coupling remains the mainstream choice for TSMC's 
$TSM
 COUPE platform and major customers (NVIDIA, AMD, Ayar Labs), with mass production expected to begin soon in 2H26.
> 耦合主流方案：光栅耦合仍是台积电 $TSM COUPE 平台及主要客户（英伟达、AMD、Ayar Labs）的主流选择，预计将于 2026 年下半年启动量产。

> PIC Capacity Ramping: TSMC plans to expand Photonic Integrated Circuit (PIC) capacity from 10k wafers per month (kwpm) in 2026 to 15kwpm in 4Q26, and to at least 25kwpm by 2028.
> 光子集成电路产能爬坡：台积电计划将光子集成电路（PIC）产能从 2026 年的每月 1 万片晶圆（kwpm）扩展至 2026 年第四季度的 1.5 万片，到 2028 年至少达到 2.5 万片。

> Customer Pipeline: Due to limited initial capacity, primary mass-production customers for 2026–2027 are expected to be NVIDIA 
$NVDA
, Broadcom 
$AVGO
, and AMD 
$AMD
. In 2028, MediaTek, Marvell, and Ayar Labs are projected to scale up.
> 客户管线：由于初期产能有限，2026 至 2027 年的主要量产客户预计为英伟达 $NVDA 、博通 $AVGO 和 AMD $AMD 。到 2028 年，联发科、Marvell 和 Ayar Labs 预计将扩大规模。

> Testing Bottleneck Easing: Wafer-level testing (Insertion 2) times have vastly improved from an entire day per wafer in 2H25 to six hours per wafer today, with a 3–4 hour target in sight for the next 6–12 months.
> 测试瓶颈缓解：晶圆级测试（插入 2）的时间已大幅缩短，从 2025 年下半年每片晶圆需一整天降至目前每片六小时，未来 6-12 个月目标为 3-4 小时。

Technology Spotlight: GlassBridge vs. Traditional FAU
技术聚焦：GlassBridge 与传统 FAU

> GlassBridge Potential: Corning’s 
$GLW
 GlassBridge (a wafer-based glass interposer solution using waveguides) offers great scaling, high density, and detachment features, but is still far from mass production.
> GlassBridge 潜力：康宁的 $GLW GlassBridge（一种基于波导的晶圆级玻璃中介层解决方案）具备出色的可扩展性、高密度和可分离特性，但距离大规模量产仍有差距。

> FAU Resilience: Traditional Fiber Array Units (FAU) remain the highly customized, lowest-loss mainstream choice. Analysts do not expect GlassBridge to disrupt high-end players like TFC in the near term, as it is more likely to compete with low-end edge-coupling FAUs first.
> FAU 韧性：传统光纤阵列单元（FAU）仍是高度定制、损耗最低的主流选择。分析师预计 GlassBridge 短期内不会冲击 TFC 等高端厂商，因其更可能首先与低端边缘耦合 FAU 竞争。
